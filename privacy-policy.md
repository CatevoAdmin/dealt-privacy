# Dealt Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 27, 2025**

## Overview

Dealt is a card game app that connects to an online service to track global game statistics and enable features like "play a solved game." This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

Dealt collects limited data to provide game tracking features:

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| Device ID | Identify your device to track your personal game statistics | Stored on our servers |
| Game Center ID | Link your stats across devices (if you're signed into Game Center) | Stored on our servers |
| Game Statistics | Games played, games won, best times, fewest moves | Stored on our servers |
| Display Name | From Game Center, for future leaderboard features | Stored on our servers |

## What We Don't Collect

- No email address or account registration required
- No location data
- No contacts or photos
- No advertising identifiers
- No third-party analytics or tracking

## How We Use Your Data

- Track which games have been solved worldwide
- Display global best times and fewest moves for each game
- Enable "play a solved game" feature
- Track your personal statistics (games played, win rate, etc.)
- Sync your progress across devices via Game Center

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or provide your data to any third parties. Your data is only used within the Dealt app and its backend services.

## Data Retention

Game statistics are retained indefinitely to maintain global leaderboards. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

Dealt does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The data we collect (device ID, game statistics) does not identify individuals personally.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, contact us at dealt@medialands.com.
