Dealt Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

Overview

Dealt is a card game app that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data Collection

Dealt does not collect any personal data. The app:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not collect analytics or usage data
  • Does not use tracking or advertising
  • Does not access your contacts, location, or other device data

Data Storage

All game data (such as settings or game progress) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Dealt does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Dealt does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at dealt@medialands.com.
