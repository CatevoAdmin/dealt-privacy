How to Play

Dealt features Baker's Game, a challenging solitaire variant similar to FreeCell. The key difference: you must build tableau stacks by same suit (not alternating colors).

  • Goal: Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, building up by suit from Ace to King
  • Tableau: Build down by same suit (e.g., 7♠ on 8♠)
  • Free Cells: Use the 4 free cells to temporarily hold cards
  • Tap: Auto-move a card to the best available spot
  • Drag: Move cards to a specific destination

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Game ID?

Each deal has a unique Game ID (seed number). The same Game ID always produces the same card arrangement, so you can replay specific deals or share them with friends.

What does the checkmark mean?

A green checkmark next to the Game ID means someone in the world has already solved that deal. You can see the global best time and fewest moves.

How do I play a solvable game?

Tap "New Game" and select "Load Solved Game" to get a deal that's been solved before. You can also enable "Only Winnable Deals" in Settings.

Does my progress sync across devices?

Yes, if you're signed into Game Center, your statistics sync automatically across all your devices.

How do I report a bug?

Go to Settings → Report a Bug and describe the issue. You can also email us directly.
